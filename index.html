About me

Hello! I'm Mateusz Bis, a dedicated and versatile Full Stack Software Engineer with a robust background in both development and design.

I' been present in the tech world since 2019 when I created Redinn, a web agency which to this day puts performance and quality at the forefront of every project. Throughout years, I've experienced working in various environments, from startups (i.a. Poznaj Góry), through middle-size companies (i.a. Optima Medical) to corporations (i.a. AVSystem).

My skill set is constantly evolving, and currently includes frontend and backend development with various frameworks listed in my résumé, devops and cloud deployment on AWS & Google Cloud. Many of my skills are proven not only by my projects shown below, but also certifications visible on my LinkedIn. Of course, my practical knowledge is accompanied by theoretical foundation thanks to my ongoing Computer Science studies at AGH UST.

In addition to my work in software engineering, I'm also a passionate photographer, I'm always on the lookout for new opportunities to capture the world around me! My photography work includes events, landscapes, portraits - all showcasing the beauty of nature and human emotions .

With a proficient knowledge of English, I excel in collaborating with international teams and clients, ensuring clear and effective communication. I'm excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and am always eager to take on new challenges! Please feel invited to scroll down and look at some of my work below.

Best regards, Mateusz Bis
