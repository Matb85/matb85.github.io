About me

Hello! I'm Mateusz Bis, a dedicated and versatile Full Stack Software Engineer with a robust background in both development and design.

I' been present in the tech world since 2019 when I created Redinn, a web agency which to this day puts performance and quality at the forefront of every project. I've experienced working in a variety of environments, from startups (Poznaj Góry), through middle-size companies (Optima Medical) to corporate settings (AVSystem).

Throughout the years, I've gained experience across a variety of domains, including development of websites, web & native apps, backend microservices, and utilization of AWS & Google Cloud. Many of my skills are not only proven by my projects shown below, but also by various certifications visible on my LinkedIn profile.

I'm also a dedicated student of Computer Science at AGH UST in Cracow, Poland, where I've been studying since October, 2023. My academic pursuits provide a strong theoretical foundation that complements my practical experience, allowing me to stay abreast of the latest advancements in the field.

With a proficient knowledge of English, I excel in collaborating with international teams and clients, ensuring clear and effective communication. I'm excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and am always eager to take on new challenges! Please feel invited to scroll down and look at some of my work below.

Best regards, Mateusz Bis
